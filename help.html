Everything you need to know about the World Index

What is Gartup?

Gartup is a minimalist neural index designed to provide clean, high-speed access to world knowledge without the visual clutter of traditional encyclopedias.

How do I search?

Simply type any topic in the main search bar on the home page. Gartup will index the most relevant node and display a distraction-free version of the information.

What are "Related Nodes"?

Nodes are interconnected pieces of information. At the bottom of every article, you will find related concepts to continue your exploration of the index.

Is there a Dark Mode?

Yes. Although the site loads in light mode by default, you can toggle the dark theme using the button in the bottom right corner to protect your eyes during late-night sessions.
